# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Employees (Staff) who book uniforms and waterpark slots; Administrators who manage inventory, quotas, and approve requests.

## Product Purpose
A centralized internal system to streamline the distribution of company uniforms and manage employee access to waterpark facilities through a quota and approval workflow.

## Brand Personality
Efficient, Professional, Reliable. The interface should feel like a high-performance tool that reduces friction for administrative tasks.

## Anti-references
Cluttered legacy ERP systems, over-complex dashboards with too many colors, and slow, non-responsive interfaces.

## Design Principles
1. **Efficiency First**: Optimize for fast data entry and quick approval workflows.
2. **Clear Quota Visibility**: Ensure users and admins can immediately see remaining balances and limits.
3. **Professional Trust**: Use a clean, consistent visual language that conveys stability and accuracy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, with a focus on high contrast for readability and clear focus states for keyboard navigation.
